## Critical Anti-Patterns (do NOT repeat these)

- [ANTI-PATTERN: silent stale-image drift] `docker compose up` does not
  rebuild when `pyproject.toml` / `uv.lock` change. → Structural mitigation:
  either always `--build` in the bootstrap script, or compare image
  create-time to `pyproject.toml` mtime before skipping. Do NOT rely on
  humans remembering to rebuild.
- [ANTI-PATTERN: bind-mount perms on non-root containers] Docker auto-creates
  missing bind-mount source dirs as root:root. Non-root containers (ZITADEL
  distroless uid 1000) then can't write. → Structural mitigation in place:
  scripts/bootstrap-dev.sh now always chowns before starting the stack.
