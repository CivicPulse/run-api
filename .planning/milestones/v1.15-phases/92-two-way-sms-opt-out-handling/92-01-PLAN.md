<threat_model>
## Trust Boundaries

| Boundary | Description |
|----------|-------------|
| Campaign-scoped message storage | Must not leak conversations across campaigns |
| Org-scoped opt-out preference store | Must not be mistaken for voice DNC or another org's SMS preference |
| Sender resolution | Must not allow sending from a number not owned by the org |

## STRIDE Threat Register

| Threat ID | Category | Component | Disposition | Mitigation Plan |
|-----------|----------|-----------|-------------|-----------------|
| T-92-01 | Information Disclosure | `sms_conversations` / `sms_messages` | mitigate | campaign RLS + unique conversation identity + explicit campaign indexes |
| T-92-02 | Tampering | `sms_opt_outs` | mitigate | org-scoped unique constraint, service-layer keyword handling, no voice DNC mutation |
| T-92-03 | Spoofing | sender resolution | mitigate | only resolve from org-owned `OrgPhoneNumber` rows with `sms_capable=true` |
</threat_model>
